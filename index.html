Our mission

NextGen HERoes empowers girls to explore, build, and lead in STEM. We create supportive spaces to practice hard skills, celebrate effort, and make professional pathways feel real and reachable.

Confidence → Action

Weekly study circles and project sprints that turn curiosity into real builds.

Community → Belonging

Peer mentorship so no one is learning alone.

Career → Access

Guest speakers, job-shadowing, and portfolio-ready challenges.

Focus areas

  • STEM Study Circles: problem-solving sessions across CS, math, physics, and chem.
  • Mini-Challenges: 1-week build sprints with showcases and prizes.
  • STEM & Gender Gap Talks: evidence-based workshops.
  • Social Campaigns: share wins, role models, and opportunities.
